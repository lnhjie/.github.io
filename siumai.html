Siu Mai (燒賣)

Fillings are of course customizable to your likings.

Catonese Dim Sum Dumplings
90 minutes
Intermediate
6 servings

Siu mai also known as a pork and shrimp dumpling is a classic dim sum dish. The traditional way is to fill it with minced pork and shrimps.

Print Recipe

Nutrition Per Serving:

  • Calories: 250
  • Fat: 12g
  • Saturated Fat: 5g
  • Sugar: 4g
  • Protein: 8g
  • Carbs: 28g

Ingredients

  • 250g Shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 150g Pork, ground
  • 50g Water chestnuts, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp Oyster sauce
  • 1 package Dumpling wrappers
  • Ginger, finely chopped
  • Green onions, chopped

Step-by-Step

  1. Mix shrimp, pork, water chestnuts, sesame oil, soy sauce, cornstarch, oyster sauce, ginger, and green onions in a bowl.
  2. Place a small spoonful of filling in the center of each dumpling wrapper.
  3. Shape the dumplings by pleating the edges and pinching the top to seal.
  4. Steam the dumplings for 10-12 minutes until cooked through.
  5. Serve with soy sauce or dipping sauce of your choice.
